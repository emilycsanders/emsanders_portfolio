About

Hi, I'm Emily Sanders! I am a junior at the University of Iowa majoring in Business Analytics and Information Systems where I will graduate with my Bachelor of Business Administration in the fall of 2025. I'm passionate about using data to drive smart business decisions and create impactful, people-centered solutions. Through my academic work and hands-on projects, I've developed skills in data analysis, strategy, and communication, which I'm eager to apply in real-world settings. I have gained experience with tools like Python, Excel, Tableau, and SQL throughout my education. I look forward to helping organizations grow through insight and innovation. Whether it's diving into a dataset or collaborating with a team, I bring curiosity, creativity, and a drive to make meaningful change.
